About Us

What Avalon is ?

Avalon logistics is a delivery service company established and managed under Avalon Group. We act as an express logistics company which provides door to door delivery service to small as well as large scale businesses , retailers , shops and also individual users throughout Nepal. We focus on the key issues of trade, commerce and logistics.

What we do ?

Avalon Logistics provides delivery service to Small and large scale businesses, individual customers, retail shops, online businesses and to those in need of a reliable and express service of goods and parcels. We provide assistance and work to fulfill your project logistics demands. We can also store your goods for further processings.

What our mission is ?

As a logistics company, our main goal is to seek ways to improve customer service and satisfaction, reduce distribution and transportation costs and eliminate factors that causes inefficiency during delivery. We are currently targeted towards the welfare of individuals who utilize our services. Most importantly we want to unite the nation throughout.

Why choose us?

  • We offer express delivery service to get your package to your customer within 24 hours.
  • We deliver to almost every nook within the valley.
  • We handle and deliver your parcel with the utmost care while maintaining your privacy.
  • We provide comprehensive and detailed tracking capabilities, allowing you to see exactly where your parcel is and has been, with minute accurate updates.
